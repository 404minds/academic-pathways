Academic Pathways is the only leading service provider that enables you to choose from a range of affiliated/approved college(s)/universities providing PG (UG as well) courses for working/non-working graduates who can/cannot afford to attend regular classes.

We provide a platform where you are just a click away from being enrolled into the course appropriate for you and your career.

We also provide a copy of approval/affiliation from the concerned university/college allotted by UGC (University Grant Commission) for the courses they run. This way you can rely on your right decision.

Well, with Academic Pathways, you will find everything you got to know before choosing your PATHWAY.
